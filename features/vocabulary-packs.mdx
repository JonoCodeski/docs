## Overview

Vocabulary Packs are curated collections of words and phrases organized by theme, difficulty level, or learning objective. Created by language experts and the MyLang community, these packs provide structured vocabulary building to complement your reading practice.

<Info>
Vocabulary Packs are currently in development. Some features may be limited while we improve the experience based on user feedback.
</Info>

## What Are Vocabulary Packs?

### Curated Collections

Each vocabulary pack contains:
- **50-500 carefully selected words** related to a specific theme
- **Difficulty-appropriate vocabulary** for your learning level
- **Context examples** showing real-world usage
- **Audio pronunciations** for proper learning
- **Cultural notes** when relevant to usage

## Browsing Vocabulary Packs

### Discovery Interface

<Steps>
  <Step title="Access Vocabulary Packs">
    Navigate to the Vocabulary Packs section from the main menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by Preferences">
    Set your source and target languages, difficulty level, and category
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse Available Packs">
    Scroll through curated collections with ratings and download counts
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview Pack Details">

  </Step>
</Steps>

### Pack Information

For each vocabulary pack, you can see:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Basic Details">
    - **Title and description**: What the pack covers
    - **Word count**: Number of words/phrases included
    - **Languages**: Source and target language pair
    - **Difficulty level**: Beginner, intermediate, or advanced
    - **Category**: Theme or subject area
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Community Metrics">
    - **Download count**: How many users have downloaded
    - **Rating**: Average user rating (1-5 stars)
    - **Reviews**: User comments and feedback
    - **Creator**: Who developed the pack (official or community)
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Content Preview">
    - **Sample words**: Preview of included vocabulary
    - **Example sentences**: Context usage examples
    - **Tags**: Additional categorization keywords
    - **Last updated**: When content was last revised
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Using Vocabulary Packs

### Download and Integration

<Steps>
  <Step title="Select and Download">
    Choose desired packs and click "Download" to add to your vocabulary
  </Step>
  <Step title="Automatic Integration">
    Words are automatically added to your vocabulary lists
  </Step>
  <Step title="Practice Integration">
    Downloaded words appear in your spaced repetition practice
  </Step>
  <Step title="Reading Enhancement">
    Pack words are highlighted when encountered in reading materials
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Practice Options

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Flashcard Practice">
    - Words from packs automatically appear in your practice sessions
    - Spaced repetition algorithm optimizes review timing
    - Progress tracking for pack-specific vocabulary
    - Difficulty ratings based on your performance
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Contextual Learning">
    - See pack words highlighted in your reading materials
    - Practice words in context from your imported content
    - Build associations between pack vocabulary and real usage
    - Track which words you encounter naturally vs. from packs
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Focused Sessions">
    - Practice only vocabulary from specific packs
    - Mix words from multiple related packs
    - Review pack vocabulary before exams or specific situations
    - Track mastery progress for individual packs
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Pack not downloading">
    **Common solutions**:
    - Check internet connection stability
    - Ensure sufficient storage space in your vocabulary
    - Try refreshing the page and downloading again
    - Contact support if error persists
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Words not appearing in practice">
    **Possible causes**:
    - Practice settings exclude pack vocabulary
    - Words already marked as "Known"
    - Daily practice limits reached

    **Solutions**:
    - Check practice preferences and filters
    - Review word familiarity levels
    - Adjust daily practice limits if needed
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Incorrect translations or contexts">
    **How to help**:
    - Use the error reporting feature
    - Provide specific details about the issue
    - Suggest corrections if you know them
    - Community feedback improves packs for everyone
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Best Practices

### Choosing Packs

<Tips>
  <Tip>
    **Start with your level** - Choose packs matching your current proficiency
  </Tip>
  <Tip>
    **Focus on relevance** - Select vocabulary you'll actually use
  </Tip>
  <Tip>
    **Balance breadth and depth** - Mix general and specialized vocabulary
  </Tip>
  <Tip>
    **Check ratings and reviews** - Learn from other users' experiences
  </Tip>
</Tips>

### Effective Practice

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Strategically">
    Don't overwhelm yourself - start with 1-2 packs at a time
  </Step>
  <Step title="Practice Regularly">
    Consistent daily practice is more effective than occasional marathons
  </Step>
  <Step title="Use in Context">
    Look for pack words in your reading materials for reinforcement
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track Progress">
    Monitor your mastery rate and adjust difficulty as needed
  </Step>
</Steps>
