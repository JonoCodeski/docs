## Best Practices

### For Beginners

1. **Start with dual mode** (Premium) or frequent translation checks
2. **Focus on high-frequency words** before rare vocabulary
3. **Read shorter content** (300-500 words) initially
4. **Use audio support** to connect written and spoken language

### For Intermediate Learners

1. **Minimize translation mode** usage, rely on word-level help
2. **Challenge yourself** with varied content types
3. **Practice inference** - try understanding from context first
4. **Track progress** with reading statistics

### For Advanced Learners

1. **Focus on specialized vocabulary** in your areas of interest
2. **Read authentic materials** like news and academic papers
3. **Use minimal highlighting** to test comprehension
4. **Export notes** for external review and analysis
