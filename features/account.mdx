## Overview

Your MyLang Reader account is the central hub for managing your learning experience, tracking progress, and customizing settings. This guide covers account creation, profile management, subscription options, and privacy controls.

## Account Creation and Login

### Getting Started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Visit MyLang Reader">
    Go to [www.mylangreader.com](https://www.mylangreader.com)
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Sign Up">
    Click "Sign Up" to create a new account
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter Details">
    Provide email address and create a secure password
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify Email">
    Check your email and click the verification link
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete Setup">
    Set your native and target languages to begin learning
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Account Troubleshooting

### Common Issues

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Login Problems">
    **Can't log in?**
    - Check email/password spelling
    - Use password reset if forgotten
    - Clear browser cache and cookies
    - Try incognito/private browsing mode
    - Contact support if issues persist
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Subscription Issues">
    **Billing or access problems?**
    - Check payment method is valid
    - Verify billing address information
    - Check email for payment confirmations
    - Allow 24 hours for subscription activation
    - Use customer portal for self-service
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Data Sync Problems">
    **Settings or progress not syncing?**
    - Ensure stable internet connection
    - Log out and back in to force sync
    - Check if using same account on all devices
    - Clear browser cache if needed
    - Contact support with specific details
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Account Recovery">
    **Lost access to account?**
    - Use "Forgot Password" to reset password
    - Check spam folder for recovery emails
    - Ensure you're using the correct email address
    - Contact support with account verification details
    - Provide approximate creation date and usage details
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Account Deletion

<Warning>
Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. Consider exporting your data first.
</Warning>

### Before You Delete

<Steps>
  <Step title="Export Your Data">
    Download all your content, vocabulary, and progress data
  </Step>
  <Step title="Cancel Subscriptions">
    End any active premium subscriptions
  </Step>
  <Step title="Consider Alternatives">
    Maybe you just need a break? Consider deactivating instead
  </Step>
  <Step title="Final Confirmation">
    Understand that deletion is permanent and irreversible
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Deletion Process

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What Gets Deleted">
    - **All account information**: Profile, settings, preferences
    - **Learning data**: Vocabulary, progress, statistics
    - **Content library**: All imported texts and documents
    - **Practice history**: Flashcard sessions and performance data
    - **Subscription data**: Billing history and payment information
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What Happens Next">
    - **Immediate effect**: Account becomes inaccessible immediately
    - **Data removal**: Complete deletion within 30 days
    - **Billing**: Any active subscriptions are cancelled
    - **Recovery period**: No recovery possible after deletion
    - **New accounts**: You can create a new account anytime
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
