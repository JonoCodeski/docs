## Overview

Your MyLang Reader Library is the central hub for all your imported content and curated reading materials. It provides powerful organization tools, progress tracking, and easy access to both your saved content and premium graded readers.

## Graded Readers (Premium)

### What Are Graded Readers?

Graded readers are specially curated texts designed for language learners:

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="Level-Appropriate" icon="signal">
    Content matched to your language proficiency level
  </Card>
  <Card title="Vocabulary Controlled" icon="brain">
    Limited vocabulary scope appropriate for each level
  </Card>
  <Card title="Cultural Context" icon="globe">
    Authentic cultural content in manageable complexity
  </Card>
  <Card title="Progressive Difficulty" icon="trending-up">
    Structured progression from beginner to advanced
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

### Difficulty Levels

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Beginner (A1-A2)">
    - **Vocabulary**: 500-1,500 most common words
    - **Sentence length**: Short, simple sentences
    - **Grammar**: Present tense, basic structures
    - **Topics**: Daily life, family, basic activities
    - **Length**: 500-2,000 words per story
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Intermediate (B1-B2)">
    - **Vocabulary**: 1,500-3,000 words
    - **Sentence length**: Moderate complexity
    - **Grammar**: Past/future tenses, conditionals
    - **Topics**: Work, travel, hobbies, opinions
    - **Length**: 2,000-5,000 words per story
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Advanced (C1-C2)">
    - **Vocabulary**: 3,000+ words, specialized terms
    - **Sentence length**: Complex, varied structures
    - **Grammar**: Advanced tenses, subjunctive
    - **Topics**: Literature, science, abstract concepts
    - **Length**: 5,000+ words per piece
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Content Management

### Viewing and Accessing

<Steps>
  <Step title="Browse Library">
    Navigate to Library section and browse available content
  </Step>
  <Step title="Preview Content">
    Click any item to see details, excerpt, and metadata
  </Step>
  <Step title="Load in Reader">

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Basic Actions">
    - **Load in Reader**: Open for reading

### Bulk Operations

Select multiple items to:
- **Delete multiple**: Clean up library efficiently
- **Tag multiple**: Apply tags to related content
- **Export selection**: Download multiple items
- **Move to collection**: Organize content groups

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Content not appearing in library">
    **Possible causes**:
    - Import process incomplete
    - Network connectivity issues
    - Browser cache problems

    **Solutions**:
    - Check import history for failed imports
    - Refresh page and check again
    - Clear browser cache
    - Try re-importing the content
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Library storage limit reached">
    **For free users**:
    - Delete unused or completed content
    - Export important content for backup
    - Consider upgrading to Premium
    - Focus on content you actively read

    **Management strategies**:
    - Regular library cleanup sessions
    - Archive completed content externally
    - Prioritize current learning materials
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Content preview not loading">
    **Common solutions**:
    - Check internet connection
    - Disable browser extensions temporarily
    - Try different browser or device
    - Contact support if issue persists
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Reading progress not syncing">
    **Troubleshooting steps**:
    - Ensure logged into same account
    - Check sync settings in preferences
    - Force sync by refreshing library
    - Verify network connectivity
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
