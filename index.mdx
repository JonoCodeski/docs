## Welcome to MyLang Reader

MyLang Reader is a powerful language learning platform that transforms your favorite content—articles, books, videos, and more—into an immersive learning experience. Whether you're a beginner or advanced learner, MyLang Reader provides the tools you need to build vocabulary, improve comprehension, and achieve fluency naturally.

## What is MyLang Reader?

MyLang Reader is a cross-platform web application that allows you to:

- **Import any content**: Articles, PDFs, ePubs, Word documents, YouTube videos, and plain text
- **Read with smart assistance**: Click any word for instant translations, definitions, and context
- **Build vocabulary naturally**: Automatically track words by familiarity level with color-coded highlighting
- **Practice with flashcards**: Spaced repetition system for long-term retention
- **Dual reading modes**: View original and translated text side by side
- **Track your progress**: Monitor your learning journey with detailed analytics

## How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="Import Content">
    Start by importing any content—paste a URL, upload a file, or select from our news feed. MyLang Reader supports articles, PDFs, ePubs, Word documents, and YouTube videos.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Read & Learn">
    Click on any word to see translations, definitions, and usage examples. Words are automatically highlighted based on your familiarity level.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Build Vocabulary">
    Mark words as learned, studying, or difficult. MyLang Reader tracks your progress and highlights known words in future content.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Practice & Retain">
    Use spaced repetition flashcards to practice vocabulary. The system adapts to your performance for optimal retention.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Supported Languages

MyLang Reader supports over 50 languages for both source and target languages, including:

- **European**: English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Dutch, Swedish, Norwegian, and more
- **Asian**: Chinese (Simplified/Traditional), Japanese, Korean, Thai, Vietnamese, Indonesian
- **Middle Eastern**: Arabic, Hebrew, Persian, Turkish
- **Slavic**: Russian, Polish, Czech, Ukrainian, Croatian, Serbian
- **And many more**: See the full list in our [language support guide](/guide/languages)
